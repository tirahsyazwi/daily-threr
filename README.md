# Daily Three 🎯

Daily Three is a minimalist, beautifully crafted productivity tool designed to help you focus on what truly matters. By limiting your daily focus to just three core priorities, you eliminate noise and reclaim your productivity.

## ✨ Features

- **The Rule of Three**: A intentional constraint to keep your focus sharp.
- **Dynamic Themes**: Choose from a variety of "vibes" to match your mood or workspace.
- **Satisfying Feedback**: Enjoy custom sound effects and confetti celebrations when you win your day.
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators show your progress through your top three.
- **Past Wins**: Keep a log of your recently completed tasks.
- **PWA Ready**: Install Daily Three on your home screen for a native app experience.
- **Crafted UI**: Built with React, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion for a fluid, premium feel.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Add your priorities**: Type your most important task in the input field and hit Enter.
2. **Focus**: Work on one task at a time.
3. **Celebrate**: Click the checkmark to complete a task. Enjoy the chime and the confetti!
4. **Repeat**: Once you've cleared your slate, you can add more, but remember—focus on three at a time.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React 18 + TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Animations**: Framer Motion (motion/react)
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Effects**: Canvas Confetti
- **Audio**: Web Audio API
